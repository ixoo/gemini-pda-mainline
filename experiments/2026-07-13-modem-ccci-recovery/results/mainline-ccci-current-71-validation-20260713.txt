Decision
--------

The vendor contract is a platform MMIO CCCI/CLDMA/CCIF modem with a 16-byte
version-3 CCCI header, 8+8 queue families, 16-byte TGPD/RGPD/TBD/RBD
descriptors, 36-bit address metadata, bootloader-owned shared memory, and
staged EMI-MPU/remap ownership. Linux `t7xx` is a PCIe/DPMAIF T700 backend;
its source compiling here does not make it a Gemini driver. Reuse belongs at
the standard WWAN/TTY/netdev API boundary, with a new MT6797 transport owning
the descriptor rings, CCIF flow-control, clocks, modem state machine, reset,
shared-memory lifetime, and EMI-MPU permissions. Keep all modem DT consumers
disabled until a non-transmitting bootloader reservation and handshake trace
is recovered.

runtime_modem_probe=not_attempted
firmware_handshake=not_attempted
wwan_transmit=none
hardware_write=none
