The Planet tree contains the complete MT6797 CPU-DVFS source inventory,
including the EEM2 calibration path that is absent from the older Gemian tree.
Linux 7.1.3's generic OPP/regulator and clock-reparenting transition helpers,
plus the newer SVS phase/error and mutable-OPP pattern, are reusable, but its
MediaTek match tables do not include MT6797. A future MT6797 variant or new
EEM/cpufreq provider is justified only after the shared EEM/thermal window,
clock, PMIC, efuse/calibration, CCI, and rollback contracts are proven. The
live table is not promoted to a board-default OPP set.
