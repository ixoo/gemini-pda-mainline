[limitations]
The AUXADC mapping reuses the existing mt8173-compatible register shape as a
compile-time candidate; MT6797 idle/wakeup semantics have not been observed.
The vendor thermal zones were disabled on the live device, so no temperature
reading, IRQ, trip, or cooling claim is made. The DT nodes remain disabled and
no MT6797 efuse provider is claimed. The patch now initializes all
`MAX_NUM_VTS` fallback slots, including `VTSABB`, before any future runtime
enablement; calibrated NVMEM data and the no-calibration policy still require
hardware validation.
