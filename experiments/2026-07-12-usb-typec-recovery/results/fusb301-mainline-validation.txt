The driver validates Device ID 0x12, configures the documented mode/current
and interrupt registers when an enabled connector node is supplied, and
reports attach, partner type, BC current, and CC orientation through the
standard Type-C class. It deliberately does not own VBUS or the Gemini
SuperSpeed redriver GPIOs. Physical port mapping, VBUS safety, and role-switch
integration remain runtime gates.
