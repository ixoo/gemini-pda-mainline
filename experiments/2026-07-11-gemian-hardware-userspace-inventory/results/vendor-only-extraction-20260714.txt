## Extraction reliability

The collector now uses explicit SSH keepalives, NUL-safe batches of 64 paths,
per-batch retries, and writes its temporary checksum file outside the payload
tree. This avoids the desktop wrapper's long single-stream timeout and the
initial run's self-hashing bug while preserving the original path allow-list
and read-only device behavior. The transferred corpus completed all ten
batches; its final manifest was regenerated with the corrected logic and
verified file-by-file.

No vendor binary was executed, no firmware was loaded, and no device state was
modified during this capture.
