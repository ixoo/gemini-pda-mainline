[decision]
Reuse Linux drivers/watchdog/mtk_wdt.c and the standard watchdog core.
The existing mt6797-wdt/mt6589-wdt compatible pair already covers the
MT6797 register protocol and TOPRGU reset-controller shape. The Gemini
board-specific missing resource is the vendor-confirmed bark IRQ: SPI137
with IRQ_TYPE_EDGE_FALLING. Keep vendor WDK/WD_API character interfaces,
SPM request side channels, modem watchdogs, and reset policy out of the
mainline ABI. Do not test start/stop/restart until an external console,
known-good boot image, and recovery owner are ready.
