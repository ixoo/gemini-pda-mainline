[decision]
Reuse the upstream BQ25890 power-supply core only after the live ID, IRQ,
battery/system-rail wiring, and safe charge limits are proven. The vendor
mediatek,sw_charger ABI and stale bq24261 DT node are not Linux bindings.
FAN49101 is a distinct buck-boost protocol: its ID registers, VSEL and
enable behavior do not establish compatibility with fan53555. Add a new
regulator driver/binding unless a verified register-level match is found.
Keep the unbound RT9466 node disabled until population and IRQ wiring are
proven; its DT values are not charge-safety evidence. Replace the vendor
battery meter/HAL with standard power_supply plus IIO/fuel-gauge pieces,
and do not copy vendor battery profiles without calibration and provenance.
